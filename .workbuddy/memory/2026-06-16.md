## Stage 1A Training Optimization — AAVE Vegas EMA 5m Cluster v0.7

**Session**: stage1-aave-vegas_ema_5m_cluster-strategy-v01-aave-2025-05-01-2026-05-30
**Iteration**: iter_001_v0.1 → v0.7

### Change
Single-rule addition to `decide()`: when the 3-timeframe directional vote produces a 1L+1S tie, and the 5m EMA stack has a clear direction (`_ema_stack_direction` returns non-None), use the 5m EMA direction as tiebreaker instead of returning FLAT. This resolves the highest-quality subset of `mixed_5m_2h_1d_directional_context` neutrals where the 5m cluster itself has structural alignment.

### Results vs v0.1 Baseline

| Metric | v0.1 | v0.7 | Δ |
|---|---|---|---|
| Directional agreement (called signals) | 51.25% | 53.69% | +2.45pp |
| Neutral rate | 12.50% | 5.67% | -6.83pp |
| Neutral-rate deviation | +5.96pp (MISALIGNED) | -0.87pp (ALIGNED) | Fixed |
| Total neutrals | 172 | 78 | -94 |
| Coverage | 87.5% | 94.3% | +6.8pp |
| Worst month | 2026-04: 37.5% | 2026-04: 39.2% | +1.7pp |

### Tradeoffs
- Skip accuracy degraded (12.8% vs 52.3%): more null-GT signals entered
- Missed neutral entries: 80 (was 0) — entering on signals with no directional resolution
- This is an inherent consequence of fixing neutral-rate misalignment; null-GT signals were correctly skipped in v0.1 but at the cost of also skipping 82 directional signals (false neutral skips)

### Versions Explored
- v0.2: 5m EMA primary (47.5%) — rejected, EMA alone unreliable
- v0.3: EMA stacks on all timeframes (50.9%) — similar to baseline
- v0.4: Required 5m EMA confirmation (42.0%) — too restrictive
- v0.5: Full tiebreaker with signal strength (53.0%, neutral MISALIGNED)
- v0.6: Filtered tiebreaker (52.8%, neutral MISALIGNED)
- v0.7: Conservative tiebreaker — 5m EMA only (53.7%, neutral ALIGNED) ← FINAL

### Key Finding
The 5m EMA cluster signal has inherently limited directional predictability (~51-54%). No single timeframe's directional read is reliable individually. The improvement from v0.7 comes from resolving the subset of mixed-context neutrals where the 5m EMA stack provides structural direction, while leaving unresolved mixed cases FLAT.
