## Stage 1A Training Optimizer Skill — Neutral Rate Alignment Upgrade

### Context
User identified that the skill had no guard against inflating directional agreement by SKIP'ing signals with valid ground truth. User proposed anchoring the strategy neutral rate to the natural null-GT rate of the signal pool.

### Changes to `skills/stage1a-training-optimizer/SKILL.md`
Added "Neutral Rate Alignment" section between Baseline Replay and Monthly Stability Audit:

- **natural_null_gt_rate**: fraction of training signals where ground truth `natural_direction` is `None`
- **strategy_neutral_rate**: fraction where `decide(...)` returns `FLAT`
- **deviation** = strategy_neutral_rate - natural_null_gt_rate
  - ±15pp: normal
  - ±15-25pp: warning
  - >±25pp: block — agreement scores unreliable

Also updated:
- Baseline Replay: added directional agreement (match/called)
- Monthly Stability Audit: added monthly strategy_neutral_rate tracking
- Training Verification: added neutral rate alignment check to required steps
- Final Response Shape: added neutral rate fields

Synced to both workspace (`skills/`) and user-level (`~/.workbuddy/skills/`).
