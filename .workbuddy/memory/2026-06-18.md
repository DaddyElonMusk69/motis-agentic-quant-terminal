### Gross PnL Column + Fee Transparency (e6fe4a8)
- User questioned whether net PnL after fee was correct (HYPE trade: ROE 24.5%, fee $8.2, net $391.8)
- Investigation confirmed the calculation IS correct:
  - Gross PnL = $400 (5% TP on $8,000 notional)
  - Fees = $8.20 (entry $4.00 + exit $4.20 at 5bps)
  - Net PnL = $400 - $8.20 = $391.80
  - ROE is NET-based: 391.80/1600 = 24.49% -> displays 24.5%
- User's confusion: assumed ROE * margin = gross win, but ROE is net (post-fee)
- Fix: added Gross PnL column to trade table, reordered to Margin | Gross | Fees | Net | ROE
- Now the fee deduction is visually verifiable: Gross $400 - Fees $8.20 = Net $391.80
