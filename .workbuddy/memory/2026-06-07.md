**Key finding**: The SHORT→LONG override (`anchor_return_pct > 0`) broke 27+ previously correct calls:
- 14 cases: truth=SHORT but override flipped to LONG (regression)
- 12 cases: truth=None (unscoreable), override created a directional call
- Broke iter_001 protected case 20260208T120000Z

**Dominant remaining failure patterns (iter_002)**:
- `bearish_pressure_with_2h_confirmation` → SHORT, truth=LONG (~90 cases)
- `bullish_pressure_with_2h_confirmation` → LONG, truth=SHORT (~68 cases)

**Two edits applied to strategy_module/strategy.py:**

1. **Narrowed SHORT→LONG override** (lines 79-92): Changed from `anchor_return_pct > 0` to `anchor_return_pct > 0.5 AND daily_last_return_pct > -0.5`. Rationale: Only override when 2h anchor is STRONGLY bullish AND the daily pullback is MILD. Strong daily pullbacks preserve the SHORT direction (like the protected `daily_softness_after_anchor_push` cases at 20260207T10-15Z).

2. **Added symmetric LONG→SHORT override** (lines 93-105): When direction=LONG AND `daily_range_position_pct >= 75` AND `daily_last_return_pct < -0.5`, override to SHORT with reason `candle_override_range_exhaustion`. Addresses the `bullish_pressure_with_2h_confirmation` MISMATCH cluster where text says bullish but market is topping out.

**Regression risk**: The narrowed SHORT→LONG threshold (0.5/0.5) should preserve the iter_002 protected cases at 20260207T10-15Z (which had daily_last < 0 with anchor_return > 0 but below the new threshold, allowing the SHORT to stand). The LONG→SHORT override risks breaking the protected 20260201T070500Z LONG case if range_position_pct >= 75 at that time — but a correct bullish signal early in the series is unlikely to have extreme range position.
